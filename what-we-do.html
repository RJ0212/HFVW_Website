Our Programs

What We Do

Comprehensive support for ventilator-dependent children and children with disabilities across the Philippines.

Our Programs & Services

From life-saving equipment to family support, we address every aspect of caring for a vent-dependent or disabled child.

Ventilator & Equipment Loans

We provide ventilators, BiPAP/CPAP machines, oxygen concentrators, and suction devices to families who cannot afford to purchase them.

Medical Supply Aid

Regular provision of tracheostomy supplies, breathing circuits, feeding tubes, diapers, and medications to vent-dependent children.

Financial Assistance

Direct financial aid for hospital bills, therapy sessions, specialist consultations, and emergency medical situations.

Caregiver Training

Training programs for parents and caregivers on ventilator care, suctioning techniques, CPR, and emergency response.

Home Visit Support

Volunteer teams visit families at home to assess needs, provide supplies, and offer emotional support and companionship.

Disability Inclusion Advocacy

Advocating for the rights and inclusion of children with disabilities, promoting accessibility, and raising public awareness.

How We Help

01

Reach Out

Families contact us through social media, referrals, or our website to share their child's needs.

02

Assess & Connect

Our volunteers assess the situation and connect the family with the right resources — equipment, supplies, or financial aid.

03

Ongoing Support

We provide continuous follow-up, home visits, and community support to ensure every vent warrior thrives.
