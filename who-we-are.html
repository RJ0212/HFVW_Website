About Us

Who We Are

A compassionate community of parents, volunteers, and advocates united for ventilator-dependent and disabled children.

Our Mission

To provide life-sustaining medical equipment, financial assistance, and emotional support to families of ventilator-dependent and disabled children in the Philippines — ensuring no child is left behind because of lack of resources.

Our Vision

A Philippines where every ventilator-dependent child has access to proper medical care, equipment, and a supportive community — where every breath is valued and every family has hope.

Our Story

Born From Love, Driven by Hope

Hope For Vent Warriors was founded by Filipino parents who know firsthand the challenges of caring for a ventilator-dependent child. What began as a small online support group grew into a nationwide charity movement.

We understand the sleepless nights, the constant worry about power outages, the struggle to afford medical supplies, and the isolation many families feel. That's why we exist — to be the support system every vent warrior family deserves.

Today, our network of volunteers, medical professionals, and generous donors works together to ensure that ventilator-dependent children and children with disabilities receive the care, equipment, and love they need.

Our Journey

  • 2018

    Group Founded

    Hope For Vent Warriors was started by a small group of parents with ventilator-dependent children in the Philippines.

  • 2019

    First Community Drive

    Organized the first donation drive, providing ventilator supplies and suction machines to 20 families.

  • 2020

    Pandemic Response

    Mobilized emergency support for vent-dependent children during COVID-19 lockdowns, delivering oxygen and supplies.

  • 2022

    Nationwide Reach

    Expanded outreach beyond Metro Manila, supporting vent warrior families across Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao.

  • 2024

    Growing Stronger

    Now serving over 500 families with equipment loans, financial aid, and community support programs.

  • 2026

    Growing Stronger

    Now serving over 1000 families with equipment loans, financial aid, and community support programs.
