Meet Our Brave Warriors

These children fight every day with courage and resilience. They are our heroes — our Vent Warriors.

Stories of Courage & Resilience

Every warrior has a story. These children and their families inspire us to keep fighting for better access to care.

Althea, 4

Tracheostomy & Ventilator-Dependent

Born with a rare airway condition, Althea has been on a home ventilator since she was 6 months old. With support from Hope For Vent Warriors, her family received a portable ventilator so she can enjoy time outdoors.

Marco, 7

Cerebral Palsy & Ventilator Support

Marco requires BiPAP support at night due to weak respiratory muscles from cerebral palsy. Our group helped his family acquire the machine and connected them with respiratory therapists.

Sofia, 2

Spinal Muscular Atrophy (SMA)

Diagnosed with SMA Type 1 as an infant, Sofia depends on a ventilator 24/7. Hope For Vent Warriors provides ongoing support for her medical supplies and suction equipment.

Elijah, 9

Chronic Lung Disease

After spending his first year in the NICU, Elijah came home on oxygen support. Our volunteers regularly visit his family, providing supplies and emotional encouragement.

Jasmine, 3

Congenital Diaphragmatic Hernia

Jasmine underwent multiple surgeries and remains ventilator-dependent. The group rallied to cover her medical expenses and provide her family with a suction machine.

Daniel, 6

Muscular Dystrophy

Daniel uses a wheelchair and nighttime ventilatory support. Hope For Vent Warriors helped fund his wheelchair and connects his parents with other families facing similar challenges.
